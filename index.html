"A solution that redefines Human-AI interactions in gaming"

ABOUT

 Our team, GAIA, aims to contribute to AI research by providing a new testing and research platform, facilitating the integration of AI models into the gaming and robotics industries.

 Our vision involves two steps: firstly, on the AI side, we plan to deploy an open-source library to share our research and work, allowing developers and researchers to utilize it. We will explore existing AI models, adapt them to our use case, and conduct training.

 Secondly, to showcase our solution, we are developing an RPG called Echo of the Dwellers with Unity that demonstrates our AI's capabilities. The goal is to create a game with complex interactions between the player, NPCs, and the environment, fostering genuine relationships based on the player's words and actions.



 In Echo of the Dwellers, the player washes ashore Tahano Island with no memories of their past. On their quest for comprehension, the player will encounter the central figures of the island, the CitAIzens. Each CitAIzen possesses the ability to speak and act autonomously, shaped by their unique perceptions and memories. As the friendship (or lack thereof) between the player and the NPCs grows, the player will acquire new capacities and learn more about the island and its inhabitants, perhaps even uncovering the reason for their presence on the island.

TIMELINE

Summer 2024

Improvement of the different player abilities and the action system for the CitAIzens, ensuring they can better behave and interact with their environment

Implementation of 4 new enemy types

Refined in game menus and UI for better user experience

Spring 2024

NPCs can now interact with the world and react physically to a player's words, including following him around, moving to a specific entity, using player designed abilities and their own inventory. If they are attacked, they will now be able to defend themselves.

Expansion of player skills, including more developed attack and parry abilities, and a way to glide and fly using tamed creatures.

Addition of more enemy types with different behaviours.

Development of a multiplateform installer so the player can easily install the game.

Winter 2023-2024

Creation of the main menu. Implementation of the first enemy and development of player skills, such as the ability to climb ladders, pick up and throw objects from an inventory, push and pull objects, and engage in parkour and bolas gameplay.

Autumn 2023

Establishment of an entity database for every in-game element, including NPCs, environment and actions. Development of a memory database to manage the CitAIzens' memories

Implementation of the first architecture allowing the server to receive perception data from Unity and generate speech for NPCs based on their memories and current perceptions.

Creation of a game design document and a dedicated lore website.

Summer 2023

In-depth research into a vector database designed to manage the memories of CitAIzen NPCs.

Implementation of a skill management system in Unity, allowing the player to jump, run and attack, as well as a time system with a day / night cycle. Creation of a debugging tool offering real-time log visualization to facilitate game development and troubleshooting.

Spring 2023

Research on AI models and benchmarking of text generation, STT and TTS models.

Development of a pathfinding algorithm in Unity.

Creation of the game's lore and characters.

Winter 2022

Development of a game demo featuring a player character and two NPCs capable of engaging in dynamic conversations through GPT-3 text generation. The game incorporates a speech recognition (STT) and synthesis (TTS) system, enabling the player to converse with the NPCs by using their microphone and receive spoken responses in real-time.
